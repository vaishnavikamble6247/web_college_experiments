Vegetable Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Capsicum
  • Salt & Spices

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Cook vegetables in a pan.
  3. Add spices and tomatoes.
  4. Mix pasta and cook for 2 minutes.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices.
  3. Add paneer cubes.
  4. Mix cream and cook for 5 minutes.
